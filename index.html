About

My name is Vasco Jorge Regal Sousa, I'm currently on my last year in Informatic Engineering (a mix of Computer Science and Software Engineering) at University of Aveiro.

Ever since I was a kid I found it fascinating how computers worked so I developed a small interest in programming and computer science even before entering university. In fact, after studying deeply various aspects that support our modern, digital world, this passion only grew stronger, especially when I realized how easy it was to transpose many of my ideas with real projects.

One of my qualities I'm most proud of is my motivation and dedication to learn new topics, be it frameworks, languages or even completly different areas, trying my best to leave my comfort bubble and challenge myself to have a more broad toolkit and set of skills.

Although I can do a bit of everything, working as a Backend Dev or messing around with DevOps activities to be the most fun and probably where I'm more skilled at.

Outsied of the tech world, my hobbies span around multiple arts, from cinema to painting. I'm a (self-proclaimed) music nerd and a huge Twin Peaks fan.
